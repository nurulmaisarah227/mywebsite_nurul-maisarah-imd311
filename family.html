My Family

In my family, there are five people: my father, my mother, my sister, me and my younger sister. My father's name is Fauzi bin Hasan and he is a policeman. He is the one who brings money home and also responsible for organizing family trips. My mother's name is Nafisah binti Hj. Ayob and she is not working. However, she is the one who is in charge for making meals and makes sure everyone eats at the appropriate times. My sister, Nur Hamizah binti Fauzi is currently studying to get a degree and my younger sister, Nor Najwa Husna binti Fauzi still schooling.
